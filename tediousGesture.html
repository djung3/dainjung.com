Tedious Gesture

Tedious Gesture is an imperfect and malfunctioning drawing machine, which seems to have no apparent reason to exist. The machine produces random and humorous drawings as it catches in a loop, where motors activate single-coil and double-coils alternately.

The initial idea of the work is to explore unexpected machine behavior provoked by communication errors between human and machine.

2015
2 stepper motors, 2 3d-printed sprockets, Coins,
Arduino microcontroller, Adafruit Motor Shield,
Blind Beaded Chain
