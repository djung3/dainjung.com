Noise

Youtube opened up new ways of communication and self-representation. Everyday, thousands of people share videos talking about private matters to a massive audience. I became curious about the act of talking as meditation and a basic human need.

Noise is intended to present the fundamental human desire to be heard and communicate. The video was originally composed of dozens of Youtube videos and later manipulated through Apple Motion software in order to explore relationships between individual and group noise.
