Gaze

Gaze explores the idea of self-consciousness, and the function of gazes on impression formation in social interactions.

The audience wears an Oculus Rift showing a crowd of human figures surrounding and staring at the audience. As the audience comes to realize that his role transfers from the observer to the observed, intangible figures invade his tangible corporeal reality, and carry the potential to evoke unexpected behavioral and emotional reactions.

2016
Oculus Rift, Headphones

Made using
Unity3d, Fuse, Mixamo
