SoundWalk

SoundWalk is an interactive audiovisual installation, which allows one to physically explore space through accidental sounds. It is an experimental attempt to gather spatial clues in reflected sounds, and will therefore help one to “make sense" of the world by drawing attention to the sonic characteristics of the city of Chicago.

The installation captures the position of the participant's head with a kinect sensor on the ceiling, which triggers several recorded sounds at any given spot. As the participant hits a certain point on the map, a sound is released toward the floor, where it reveals history of a site and oneself, presenting an ever-changing kinetic display that blends the individuals’ infinitely variable and unique movement.

2015
Laser projecter, Kinect sensor, Parabolic speaker

Made using
Openframeworks
