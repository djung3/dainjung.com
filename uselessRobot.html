Tedious Gesture

Machines are ideally made to assist us, to make our life easier.  However, what if there is a useless machine?

Tedious Gesture is an imperfect and malfunctioned drawing machine, which seems to have no apparent reason to be existed. The machine produces random and humorous drawings as it caught in a loop, where two motors’ activate single-coil and double-coils alternatively.

The initial idea of the work is to explore unexpected machine behavior provoked by communication error between human and machine.

2015
2 stepper motors, 2 3d-printed sprockets, Coins,
Arduino microcontroller, Adafruit Motor Shield,
Blind Beaded Chain
