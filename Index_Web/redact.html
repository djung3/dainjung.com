Redact

Redact questions and proposes a new way of reading. How the user’s behaviors (choice and action) remap one’s experience of reading the "Poetry"?

As the user tapped the words, each words from original texts blacked out and creating poetic subtexts. Redact allows users to gain a prominent and active role in creating visual poetry and shaping distinctive reading experience from a limited word bank.
