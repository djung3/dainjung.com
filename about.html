about

Da In Jung is a visual artist whose work is mainly driven by a sense of curiosity, realization, conflict and ongoing studio practice. Her creative practice is fundamentally focus on multifaceted human relationship and its technologies, and the ways these relationships can approach new art forms. Her work takes the form of virtual and augmented reality art installations, kinetic sculpture, interactive installation and moving images.
